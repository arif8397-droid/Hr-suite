Bangladesh Labour Act 2006 (Amended 2018) Minimum Entitlements:
Earned Leave: 1 day per 18 worked days (≈11/yr). Sick Leave: 14 days/yr (fully paid). Casual Leave: 10 days/yr. Maternity: 112 days paid (for 8+ months service). Festival Holidays: 11 gazetted days.
Leave Quotas (days/year)
🔒 Annual Leave is auto-calculated from attendance (1 day per 18 working days) — no quota needed.
∞ Special Leave and Compensatory Leave have no quota — they are unlimited.
